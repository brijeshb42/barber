Initialization

  • Define a few image sizes from the Sizes link if there aren't any.
  • The name of the size is very important. It is used when naming images after uploading. Do not include any other character except alphabets in the name.
  • Provide a valid Width and Height.
  • These are used to maintain the aspect ratio of the selected image area while cropping.

Selecting desired image

  • If you have the image URL, paste it into the input box and press enter.
  • OR, drop the image from your file explorer of PC onto the designated area.
  • OR, browse your PC for image by clicking the Choose file button.

Free size

  • Free size button allows you to select your desired region of image without any restriction on aspect ratio.
  • For ex, to just upload an image from anywhere to our own server, just input the image image URL, click on Free button and resize the crop area to fit the entire image and then click Save.
  • If you want the free size selection to resize to a particular width, just enter the desired width into the Resize Width input. Height is automatically calculated based on the aspect ratio of the selected area.

After selecting image

  • When you click on a predefined size button, the crop area appears. Move it around or resize the area to get your desired region of image.
  • Select other size regions.
  • After completing the selection, confirm the sizes you want to save by checking the relevant boxes.
  • Now click on Save and wait. Image manipulation is a time taking operation.
  • Once the resizing is done, press OK. The relevant image URLs will be displayed which can be copied by clicking the Copy button.
